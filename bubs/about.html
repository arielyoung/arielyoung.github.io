Bubs Bubba Paco Simba

Bubs was brought into my life in September of 2001. He was a hyper little fella. He was as white as snow and could get lost during the winter storms. He was a fluffy pom. He was loved, treated like a king. He lived a long life, 15 years. He was spoiled, plentiful treats, beds and toys. But he always chose to sleep in front of the door. Like a guard dog, standing at 14lbs. Its like he never slept during the earlier years. But as time went on, all he could do was lay down. He had no strength to stand up and would plop down when he legs gave out. He loved to eat human food (cause what dog wouldn't) and refused to eat dog food. Eventually we had to stop and give him some healthy dog food. For his birthdays he got plenty of doggy cupcakes and treats. Family dinners he got turkey and whatever he could eat. Yep. he was great. Always a smile on his face. Barking away at the walls when he sensed something wrong. Scratching at our bedroom doors for attention. Hiding in my room during the fourth of july. Yep it was great. My dearly beloved bubs.
